Whey Protein

Description

Whey protein is a mixture of proteins isolated from whey, which is the liquid part of milk that separates during cheese production.

Ingredients

  • Casein
  • Whey

Steps

  1. After being separated during cheese production, whey goes through various processing steps to become what people generally recognize as whey protein — a powder that is added to shakes, meal replacements and protein bars (5).
  2. It’s usually sold as a flavored powder, which is added to shakes, meal replacements and protein bars.
